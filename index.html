About DreamMore

Who We Are

DreamMore is a technology-driven educational and creative company founded by passionate young innovators in Ethiopia. We connect talented teachers with motivated students to deliver high-impact training in web development, graphic design, video editing, and basic computer skills. Our goal is to inspire and empower the next generation of tech leaders.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between learning and employment by delivering accessible, practical, and high-quality training programs that unlock students’ potential and prepare them for the digital economy.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia’s most trusted youth-led education company, building a future where every student has the digital skills to succeed, innovate, and lead with confidence across industries.
